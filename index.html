About Us

ThinkZone is an ecosystem that supports entrepreneurship and innovation with 3 divisions. The first division is ThinkZone Innovation Lab including Startup Academy - to equip startups with a comprehensive set of expertise and skills, and Corporate Innovation - to elevate innovation in corporates. The second division is ThinkZone Accelerator, an acceleration program where we select and leverage potential tech startups. Last but not least, ThinkZone Ventures is an early-stage capital firm for ambitious founders to raise funds for their businesses.
