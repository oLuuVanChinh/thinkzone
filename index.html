About us

ThinkZone is an ecosystem that supports entrepreneurship and innovation with 3 divisions. The first division is ThinkZone Innovation Lab including Startup Academy - to equip startups with comprehensive set of expertise and skills, and Corporate Innovation - to elevate innovation in corporates. The second division is ThinkZone Accelerator, an acceleration program where we select and leverage potential tech startups. Last but not least, ThinkZone Ventures is an early-stage capital firm for ambitious founders to raise funds for their businesses.

Our mission

ThinkZone mission is to nurture a generation of resilient, skillful and passionate entrepreneurs for Vietnam in the time of global economic inclusion.

Our vision

We aim to become the top of mind company when it comes to coaching and bringing startups to the global level.
